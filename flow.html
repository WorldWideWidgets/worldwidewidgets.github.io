This view treats the blockchain as a pipe system. Arrows indicate the direction of capital flow. Thick lines represent high volume transfers. Again - these data are from month (Feb. 2026) on coinbase-44 and the currency we're looking at is Etherium (ETH). For refernce, in April 2026 the conversion rate to a well known fiat currency is 1 ETH == $2000 USD. There are a lot of trades on coinbase, so I have the zoom pretty far out, the thresholds pretty high. Feel free to adjust.

Look for Funneling: many inputs leading to a single output. Or Peacocking: a single input splitting into many outputs.
